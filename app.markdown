# Get Alerts
Sophie pushes out notifications, like Amber Alerts, directly to your phone. You can get the usernames of pedophiles forwarded to you, all in the app.

## Write Reports
You can submit a report in-app. Reports are sent to law enforcement, and your personal information is never included.
You can also report right here: [File A Report](https://forms.gle/zuc7mtz8EycFhf1a8) (You are subject to Google's Privacy Policy, for now, we are working on making an in-site form with better privacy)

Who is Sophie?

Sophie is a bot that is designed to detect pedophile activity in your server and alert everyone to it. It does this by detecting common phrases said by pedophiles. In extreme cases these messages are sent to us, and if we determine that person is a pedophile we will put them in our blacklist, where they cannot join any servers Sophie is in.

Auto Detection

Sophie looks for messages that looks like grooming, and if the user in question is older than 18 in her database she throws a red flag.

Blacklist

We work with pedophile hunters and several communities to gather as many pedophiles' IDs as possible, building an effective blacklist.

Verified Users

If we have verified a users' age and identity, Sophie will make sure their messages are marked as verified. Talk with peace of mind.

1 in 10 children will be victimized.

According to Dark to Light - a resource to help prevent the exploitation of children - 1 in 10 children will be victims of sexual assault. 1 in 7 girls will be sexually assaulted, and 1 in 25 boys will be sexually assaulted. According to Victims Of Crime, a child that is under prolonged sexual abuse can become suicidal and withdrawn. Children with broken-up parents, children subject to verbal or physical abuse, and children dealing with domestic violence have a higher change of being victimized.

Tools for parents and educators alike

Sophie has plenty of tools for parents and educators. Have you ever tried setting up parental controls on a child's device? It can be painstaking, and sometimes they won't work as expected! Sophie's AI allows her to accurately detect explicit content and block it - on any site. Instead of banning sites, just ban the content. Set what level you want and be done! Let your child browse the web with peace of mind.

Teachers? Sophie offers a database of pedophiles and their information to schools and educators, as well as our expanded Resource Center to help counselors work with victims.

What we will do

Sophie isn't just some startup, she isn't just some AI. She is an AI powered by Tensorflow - a framework developed by the AI Powerhouse that is Google. We aren't just an AI, though, we are an entire community. We plan to raise awareness, spread education, as well as stunt the reach that pedophile's currently have. We don't just want to ban pedophiles from Discord servers, we want to start a revolution that is a long time coming.

Who we are

We aren't just Sophie Bot anymore, we are an entire team dedicated to starting an internet revolution for an internet without pedophiles.

Our Lineup

Sophie Bot

Free

Sophie Bot is the powerful Discord Bot. If you are the owner of a Discord server and want a flexible way to protect your userbase - this is the place to go. Free for both commercial and personal use.

>> Source Code

Sophie AI

Free*

Sophie AI is the powerful AI behind Sophie Bot. Powered by the Sophie API, you can make requests to the AI and find out how explicit certain messages are, based on the Sophie Scale. From there, you can determine whether or not to censor a message on your chat board, in-game chat, or more. The possibilities are endless! Free for personal use, for commercial use please contact us.

SophieDB

Free*

SophieDB is a limited representation of the data Sophie collects. You can send in a username, IP Address, or other information and in return you'll get their standing with Sophie and if they are flagged as a pedophile. You will never receive an IP address, username, address, age, or location from SophieDB - even if we have the information.

Sophie Extension

Free

Ready for any web browser you may have, Sophie Extension is a browser extension that uses Sophie's AI to determine if text is sexually explicit, and if it is it will be censored. This is perfect for parents that want to filter their child's communication, schools that want to limit student's exposure to explicit content, or teenagers that are tired of pedophiles always hitting them up.

Sophie App

Free

Free for Android and Windows, Sophie App sends notifications whenever a Pedophile Alert (PA) is issued from Sophie Team, and has a list of all pedophiles that are explicitly flagged by Sophie Team. The app does not contain information on individuals Sophie's AI flags as suspicious, but people that have been verified by our human moderation team to be a danger to children and teenagers.

At this time we do not have an iOS app as we cannot compile the code to publish it. This will change soon.
